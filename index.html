AITIASCOPE

Aitiascope is an Final Fanstasy XIV Roleplay Community

Building a Community

Aitiascope is a curated community that uses elements of a roleplay group structure. We set universe timelines, world limits, and setting limitations to help roleplayers confer to the same standard.

Roleplay

Aitiascope is an organized projectmeant to help connect and organize events in the world that give incentives for individuals, groups, and communities to roleplay together. While the aim is to connect the greater community together through the setting, our core goal is to expand, expose, and connect the many avenues of roleplay within final fantasy 14.

Take a look at the setting.

Limitations

We do not limit your ability to roleplay outside of this community, in fact we want to encourage roleplayers that participate to continue their plots, storylines, and other avenues of story telling outside of the community. the limitation we set are for community events only. Outside of those you are free to curate your experience as you please.

Review the rules.
