Aitiascope: Rules

Guidelines and restrictions

General Guidelines

The base guidelines all should follow.

Respect other Members

When you join you are agreeing to show the base level of respect towards other members.

RP around your schedule

Some roleplays or events may be shorter or longer. Be aware of this and how often you plan to RP before engaging in a serious or long standing plot, or find graceful ways to remove yourself from the plot. For emergencies, please contact the GM so that other players can continue the plot.

Stay IC in IC areas

Chats can easily become cluttered in game so being mindful of posting any OOC messages is important. Use tells or party chats to handle OOC messages between players.

Stick to the setting when you are in the setting

When you are roleplaying in the setting or setting events, only events within the setting should be mentioned or referred to. Your RP and events outside of the setting are yours to curate and have but they must never be brought into the setting.

Common Sense & Maturity

Whether it comes to conflict resolution or simply chatting between members we expect common sense and maturity to be used in every situation.

Growth & Stability

If it needs change it will be changed. As this setting develops more rules may be added. We expect members to adhere to these new rules as they are made aware of them.

Prohibited Behaviors

These behaviors, if performed maliciously or continuously after warning, will get a member removed from the community.

God-Modding

God modding is when a character features god-like abilities such as invincibility, mind control, or other unrealistic powers that might not fit in with the setting.

Metagaming

While you are free to discuss with other members the plots or occurrences, this knowledge cannot be applied to your character. If your character was not there, they don't know and it cannot be applied to your roleplays. We recommend that you keep IC information to yourself and resist sharing, however we cannot control what you share in private conversations.

Auto

Auto is when you perform an action without giving the affected players a chance to respond. Your actions should detail their characters actions, but your actions towards other characters should be worded as attempts.

Powerplay

You are not allowed to operate someone else's character without their consent. What you write should always be in reference to your own character and their actions unless agreed otherwise.

Community Guidelines

Understanding the Setting restrictions

Joining doesn't mean you are no longer allowed to run your venues, host your own RP events, or continue to operate in the independent role-player space in FFXIV. When you join this group, you are agreeing to keep that and your group activities separate. Setting events will not be advertised in the party finder, and many of the events will be held in public spaces. Should anyone ask or request information about the setting, feel free to share and if they want to apply, please feel free to point them in the right direction. However, the setting is separate.

This setting does not aim to restrict you from any outside freedoms. You should not stop doing your FC events, your venues, or anything like that, but we ask that you respect the space we created with the setting and the boundaries placed. Any connections you mke within the setting you are free to foster outside of it as well.

The Benefits & Honor System

There is no gil. No monetary gain. No deeper incentive beyond roleplay. When you join you're joining because you just like roleplaying, you like writing and you want to write with other like minded RPers. The only thing joining will cost you is some time and effort.

There will always be a way to game the system in place but with the understanding that the goal is to roleplay, we expect that the time and effort put forth is to enhance your roleplay experience. Foul play will alwyas be looked into, but understandably absuing the system will only hurt your experience and the others around you.

Characters

This community will ask, upon application, for characters to meet a certain set of limitations in regards to power level, class, influence, and so on. When you join, it should be considered an alternate universe to your personal or existing. It can have elements of your personal story for your character but we aim to level the playing field for all characters that join. It should be a group of normal (relative to the universe) people.

We won't accept gods, deities, ancients, overpowered characters, ascians, iseka'd characters. We will deny npcs and you may not elect to be the Warrior of Light in any aspect. You may be affiliated with in game groups such as the Scions of the Seventh Dawn or a Grand company but we will consider these affiliations under a set of limitations that we do not want to impact the realism or immersion of other players.

Your character must be from the universe in which we exist in (Final Fantasy XIV). we will deny troll name characters. Of note, We do not consider your in-game name under this but the name you are using in character. For example, if your FFXIV character name is "Ben Dover" or "G'raha Tia", but your character name in setting is lore compliant, then that is okay. But we will reject both Ben Dover and G'raha Tia as character names.

Groups & Affliations

A group of characters from an FC may join together with the intention of being a group in this setting, this is NOT prohibited. However, you are not required to be in an FC or join an FC to be a part of this setting, nor does being in one and wanting to join unaffiliated effect your ability to join. The groups and factions created in this setting are isolated from your actual groups in game.

Discord

Because of the nature of discord, this community will not have one. We believe forums and discussion allow for longer disscussions, and the ability to peopl eto browse toics and subjects of interest. Free to create discord servers of your own for any varied purpose however any discord servers made are not moderated or affiliated with this moderation team.
