Aitiascope: Setting

Understanding the Universe

World State

Aitiascope uses world state to create a cohesive coundation for the universe that characters will exist in. It is a way for the players to understand not only the timeline, but the corresponding world events that impact the current state of the universe.

The Rebellions

For 1.0, the world state is set in at Patch 4.0 in Final Fantasy 14. Admist the attempted liberation of Ala Mhigo from the Garlean Empire and its subsequent failure, the Eorzean Alliance has initiated a call for aid. Countrymen across the three great continents are requested to come together to supply the liberation effort with aid.

Businesses, Orginizations, Free Comapnies and individuals are all called to action to support the liberation by fulfilling the requests of the Eorzean Alliance. However, this will require coordination, support and supplies of the different regions to contribute to the greater effort. Trade, deal, sell and organize with other players to contribute to the efforts of the liberation and rise to prominence.

Player Contributions

Each corresponding region will have a resource used for contributing to the world state system. These resources are purposely picked for trading and sharing among different business, organizations, free companies and individuals. Anyone can turn in resources, but they must be attributed to a faction or free agent. There contributions will be tracked and used to cause changes or effects to the world state.

These items will be announed at the start of the month. The items will be validated by name (the player that crafted it), and attributed to a player or group and the region of the player or group. For example, if the alliance needs 1000 longs of Elm Lumber, players can contribute to the cause by crafting and turning in Elm Lumber. The Elm Lumber will be only verified that the name on the item is a member of the community or their alternate character.

Available regions: Othard, Kugane, Dravania, Coerthas, Vylbrand, The Black Shroud, Xelphatol, Thanalan

Neutral Areas: Mor Dhona, Thavnair
