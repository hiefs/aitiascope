Aitiascope: The Setting

Understanding the Universe

World State

Aitiascope uses world state to commuicate the universe that the character exist in. It is a way for the players to understand not only the timeline, but the corresponding world events that impact the current state of the universe.

Aitiascope 1.0: the Rebellions

For Aitiascope 1.0, the world state is set in at Patch 4.0 in Final Fantasy 14. Admist the attempted liberation and failure of Ala Mhigo from the Garlean empire, the Eorzean Alliance has initiated a call for aid. Countrymen across the three great continents are requested to come together to supply the liberation effort with aid.

Businesses, Orginizations, Free Comapnies and individuals are all called to action to support the liberation by fulfilling the requests of the Eorzean Alliance. However, thi will require the support and supplies of many different regions to contribute to the greater effort.

Player Contributions

Each corresponding region will have a resource used for contributing to the world state system. These resources are purposely picked for trading and sharing among different business, organizations, free companies and individuals. Anyone can turn in resources, but they must be attributed to a faction or free agent. There contributions will be tracked and used to cause changes or effects to the world state.

Available regions: Othard, Kugane, Dravania, Coerthas, Vylbrand, The Black Shroud, Xelphatol, Thanalan

Neutral Areas: Mor Dhona (Neutral Area) Thavnair (Neutral Area)
